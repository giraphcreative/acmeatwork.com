Did you know ACME Federal Credit Union provides tools to promote financial wellness for your employees?

By making your employees’ lives better financially, we can help reduce stress at home and at work. Studies show this creates better retention, reduced absenteeism, and increased productivity.

Statistics from:
1 2016 PWC Financial Wellness Report
2 Federal Reserve Study, September 2014
3 The National Foundation for Credit Counseling (NFCC) 2017 Consumer Financial Literacy Survey

ACME Federal Credit Union is a member-owned financial cooperative dedicated to people, not profit. We’re here to help your employees get more for their money. That means lower loan rates, higher savings yields and fewer fees and charges.

You can expect all the conveniences offered by a big bank, and the service you deserve from a not-for-profit credit union.

We offer free benefits and resources for your employees such as:

  • Replacement 401K loan options
  • Mortgage/ Home Equity Loans
  • New & Used Auto Loans
  • Health Savings Accounts
  • Financial wellness resources
  • Onsite visits and seminars*
  • Credit Counseling

*Onsite visits and seminars available to northeast Ohio SEGs only.
